About Me

Coder of the Web and Devourer of all the Ice Cream

Hi, I'm Joey Nguyen. Welcome to my personal website! I'm currently a front end developer at Dealertrack Technologies in Dallas, TX where I work on a team that's building a new and improved web app for our business customers, following the Agile (Scrum) methodology.
